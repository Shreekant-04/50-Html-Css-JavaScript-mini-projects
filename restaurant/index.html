About Us

Welcome to Spice Haven, nestled in Connaught Place, New Delhi. We create memorable dining experiences with our cozy ambiance and fresh, local ingredients. Whether for a casual lunch or a celebratory dinner, our dedicated team ensures you feel at home. Join us for a taste of culinary excellence.

Our Menu

Spice Haven offers a diverse menu inspired by global flavors. From savory appetizers to delectable desserts, our dishes are crafted with the freshest ingredients. We cater to all dietary preferences, including vegetarian and gluten-free options. Discover your new favorite dish with us.

Our Chefs

Our culinary team, led by Executive Chef Arjun Mehra, brings international expertise and creativity to Spice Haven. Each dish is a masterpiece, blending tradition and innovation. Meet our talented chefs and experience the artistry that makes dining with us truly exceptional.
